2. Data We Collect

2.1 Search Queries

When you use the AI Search feature, your search queries are processed to generate responses. Query text, timestamps, and response quality metrics are stored in our analytics database to improve service quality.

If you access the administration interface, a session cookie (admin_session) is set to authenticate your session. This cookie is strictly necessary for admin functionality and does not track public users.

2.3 Anonymized Support Data

Our knowledge base includes anonymized support email threads. All personally identifiable information (names, email addresses, serial numbers) is replaced with placeholders before any AI processing occurs.

2.4 Cookie Consent Preference

Your cookie preference is stored in your browser's local storage to remember your choice.
